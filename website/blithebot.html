This isn't sentiment tagging — it's adversarial reasoning applied to every signal, at machine speed, with no cognitive bias.

Position Sizing

Half-Kelly Sizing.
Trained on your own edge.

Every position is sized using a Kelly Criterion model trained on Blithebot's live trade history — win rate and payoff ratio drawn from the signal_outcomes table. A minimum of 20 trades is required before Kelly activates per strategy. Hard cap at 10% per position. Portfolio heat enforced above Kelly.

Positions scale up as the bot proves itself on a given setup and scale back when the statistical edge weakens.

Circuit Breakers

Strategies self-pause
on drawdown.

Each strategy maintains its own circuit breaker. A 5% drawdown within a 10-day window triggers automatic pause. The breaker resets on recovery — no human intervention required. Full audit trail logged to PostgreSQL.

Exit Architecture

Exits are systematic.
Never discretionary.

All open positions are monitored by a dedicated trailing stop loop running continuously. ATR-based trailing stops update on each new bar. A separate exit monitor loop backtracks P&L on closed positions and writes outcomes to the signal_outcomes training table.

Three Strategy Families

Independent alpha.
Unified risk management.

Each strategy runs in its own loop with its own circuit breaker, parameter set, and position envelope. Uncorrelated signals by design.

Overnight Discovery Engine

The bot that improves
while markets sleep.

Every night after close, the Discovery Engine runs automated research on the top 100 S&P 500 symbols by 30-day average volume. It tests thousands of parameter combinations, evolves novel indicators via genetic programming, and graduates only those that pass a rigorous walk-forward IC threshold.

01

Symbol Universe Selection

Top 100 S&P 500 symbols ranked by 30-day average volume are selected each cycle. The universe refreshes via a dedicated async loop to reflect current liquidity conditions.

02

Walk-Forward Grid Search (v1)

243 parameter combinations across EMA periods, RSI thresholds, and MACD settings tested against 4-month rolling walk-forward windows. A scipy t-test filters results to those with statistically significant edge.

03

Multi-Family Expansion (v2)

Discovery Engine v2 extends to 5 strategy families across the top-100 universe. Walk-forward IC scores computed per family per symbol. Approved results stored as JSONB and surfaced via Streamlit dashboard for review.

04

Genetic Programming Engine

A 50-population, 20-generation GP engine evolves novel indicator expressions from a standard technical primitive set. Fitness measured by Information Coefficient. IC > 0.05 required to graduate.

05

Regime-Adaptive Deployment

The Regime Adapter selects the best approved strategy per symbol based on the current SPY regime — trending, mean-reverting, or random. Live parameters update without human intervention.
